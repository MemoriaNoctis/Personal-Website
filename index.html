About

Let me introduce myself!

I'm a third year student pursuing an Honours Specialization in Interdisciplinary Medical Sciences at the University of Western Ontario. And while I love learning about medicine, the pandemic has made me realize that this path wasn't the best for me. So now I'm also pursuing a major in computer sciences, and I'm halfway through my second year.

Why tech, you ask? Well, I enjoy finding solutions to problems, which is partially why I wanted to work with patients. Coding isn't much different, except instead of the pressure of "troubleshooting" on living beings, we're doing it on machines (that's less terrifying, I'd say).

I think that my HSP + Major combo is a testament to my work ethic and potential. My contributions in clubs and my previous service job can also show that I pull my weight and am a good collaborator. While subjective qualities are hard to demonstrate in a few paragraphs, I'm more than willing to prove it if you give me a chance.
